# `use/` — area contract

**Job:** use and self-host Tapestry.

**Belongs:** tutorials, how-to guides, reference, concepts, operating your own
deployment (`operate/`), troubleshooting (`troubleshoot/`), release notes &
migrations (`releases/`).

**Does NOT belong:**
- the *decision* to build a feature → `decide/`
- our internal managed-service runbooks → private `run/`
- durable product intent → `direction/`

**Override:** `operate/` and `troubleshoot/` set `review_cadence: 90d` (decay-prone).
